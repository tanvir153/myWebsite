Who Am I?

I'm MD Tanvir Sardarr a passionate data analyst and aspiring machine learning engineer with a solid foundation in software engineering and a growing specialization in data science. My current academic pursuit at the University of Chester, paired with prior industry experience, has shaped me into a well-rounded problem solver with both strategic vision and technical execution.

I hold a Bachelor's degree in Software Engineering from Zhengzhou University, China, where I graduated with distinction and got out standing student award. My journey through data began with programming, blossomed through database systems and AI, and matured into practical work involving real-world datasets, predictive analytics, and insightful visual storytelling.

Over the years, I’ve developed and deployed student dropout prediction tools, hospital database systems, Power BI dashboards, and customer churn models each grounded in Python, SQL, and machine learning principles. Comfortable across the full data pipeline, I’m especially passionate about using data to promote educational equity.

I’m multilingual. Fluent in Bengali, English, Mandarin, and conversational in Japanese. I thrive in multicultural environments and enjoy learning from global perspectives. My hobbies include playing badminton, sketching UI/UX ideas, studying languages, and exploring futuristic technologies in AI and robotics.

Ultimately, I see myself at the intersection of intelligent systems and impactful human experiences, solving problems that matter using data, algorithms, and creativity.
